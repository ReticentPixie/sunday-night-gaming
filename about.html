About Us

What is Shameful Display Gaming?
Shameful Display Gaming is a private Discord Server where friends from across the country gather to play games of all types on all platforms. There's also some shitposting, lots of mentorship, and general community shenanigans.
Meet Our Bot
ShameBot is a multipurpose bot built and maintained by our server members. ShameBot is an evergrowing bot built primarily in nodeJS. Check out the github repo here to learn more.
